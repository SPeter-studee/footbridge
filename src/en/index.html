Methodology

What we do differently

01

We go to the root, not the symptom

A process problem, after 5 Whys, usually isn't at the operational level — there's a leadership decision or measurement gap behind it. That's where intervention makes sense.

02

We look before we leap

If your team spends 14 hours a week on invoice processing, that doesn't necessarily mean you need to automate it — you might not need the process at all. That question saves time and money.

03

We measure whether it was worth it

ROI, payback period, hours saved — the context determines the metric. We never talk about results in the abstract.

04

One process at a time, not the whole company

The first bridge is 3 weeks, fixed cost, measurable result. The full transformation comes later, once the small bridges are built.
